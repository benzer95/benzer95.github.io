Quest Log

Session 1 - Bandit Camp

Notable enemies defeated: Gorthag the Great, Gorthag the Godly, Dave the Bandit King
Notable loot obtained: Tricksy rope, 500 coin
On a quest to rescue Baldric's stolen mandolin (a mandolin that was passed down to him by a long family line of unsucceful bards), our heroes tracked the culprits to a cave inhabited by bandits. Inside they fought bandits lead by Gorthag the Great, a spear and dagger weilding warrior. Gorthag the Great was killed by Kronk; Kronk shot Gorthag in the eye while doing a backflip.
Further in the cave the heroes encountered Gorthag's brother, Gorthag the Godly. Kronk eliminated Gorthag by knocking him into the dark rushing waters below from a narrow rock bridge, after Gorthag got himself into the situation where he was hanging onto Baldric's legs for dear life. Baldric himself was hanging onto Thrandian, who was in turn hanging onto the ledge of the bridge.
Finally the heroes defeated Dave the Bandit King, by shooting him in the gut, headbutting him as a goat, and then chopping off his head. Dave had believed that Baldric's mandolin was magical (it's not) and had it stolen, so it was here where Baldric was reunited with his mandolin (as well as 500 coin).
We last left our heroes heading to the lake town of Lamora, to rest and sell a wagon full of salted beef and fish.
